---
title: "Start with a Why"
date: "2024-12-18"
---

Sometimes I find myself doing things for their own sake. Not for any particular reason, without anything in mind, just doing. 

Why am I writing this? Why code this site? Why not study for my physics final exam I have in two days? This questions are all right to ask and each answer helps the other. Ill try my best to answer (and understand myself) why am I coding/writing this.

The idea is to use this as a sharing place. I want to express my thoughts, show what I create, and try to be useful for other people in the way.
